What do we do?

We created such website in order to simplify tasks of enrolling into course by making it online. Because, usually people are not able to get info about one school without visiting it and studying there for about a month. This takes much time which is priceless resource for pupils at the time of preparation. So our website enables users to see details about course and other info such as teachers, course prices, what subjects it contain, lesson’s duration, our contact numbers, addresses online. It is free for use, and affordable for everyone who has access to the internet. In the main page people can see the video from youtube about our course which is essential for any school. Our website also eases tasks of this school’s owners. Because making those processes online saves a lot of time and money. Pupils can enrol themselves to trial lesson.



In the 1'st page




First page is a main page that is opened on click of our url. So we made it attractive and understandable for everyone. It contains info about our courses, in the header we have buttons to transfer between pages, then a button to enroll yourself for a trial lesson, if you scroll down you can watch a video in youtube about our course which is important to be in first page, then prices of courses and in the bottom we placed our logo and contacts.









In the 2'nd page




Second page is opened by clicking “Courses” button in the header. It mostly contains details about course subjects. There you can face 7 different subjects and header also. First three subjects are mandatory to choose and other four in the bottom are extra subjects, pupil choose two of them for preparation. And in the bottom again you can see logo and contacts.



In the 3'rd page




Third page will appear on clicking to the contacts button. It has detailed information about our address and contact numbers, social networking accounts’ links and mails. For map we used google maps to make it understandable for everyone, if you click on it it opens a route to our schools building, also people can write a review about our course in this field and read feedback of others.









In the fourth page




Fourth page is a sign in page. It has header as other pages, and username and password fields where we used inputs, and in the password field password that are written are hidden by star sign to make it safer. Then we have hyperlink with “Forgot password” text which will be used by people who forgot their passwords. And then we have a button “Login” which is apparently for logging in.
